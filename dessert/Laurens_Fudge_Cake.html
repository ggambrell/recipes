Lauren's Fudge Cake

Opinion: Love

Based on: "https://myconsciouseating.com/lindas-fudge-cake-recipe/"

Active - 15 min :: Total - 1 hr

Serves: 12

Equipment:

  • cake pan
  • 2 mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer
  • Wire rack
  • Double boiler or microwave-safe bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÃÂ°F (180ÃÂ°C). Prepare two cake pans.
  2. Whisk together dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, combine eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat with electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Gradually add wet ingredients to dry ingredients while mixing on low speed until smooth.
  5. Stir in hot water until just incorporated (batter will be thin).
  6. Divide batter evenly between pans. Bake 35-40 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pans 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. For frosting: melt chocolate chips and butter until smooth.
  9. In a separate bowl, mix powdered sugar, heavy cream, sour cream, and vanilla.
  10. Gradually mix melted chocolate mixture into the creamy mixture until smooth.
  11. Frost cake: spread frosting on one layer, add second layer, and cover entire cake with remaining frosting.
  12. Optional: garnish with chocolate sprinkles or drizzle of chocolate sauce.

Notes:

  • Use high-quality cocoa powder for deeper chocolate flavor.
  • Do not overmix the batter; mix until just combined.
  • Store tightly wrapped in the refrigerator up to 5 days; freeze up to 3 months.
  • Serve slightly chilled for best texture.
