Chocolate Chip Icecream

Opinion: Bad, Good, Love

Based on: "https://www.food.com/recipe/chocolate-chip-ice-cream-459455"

Active - 5 min :: Total - 20 min

Serves: 4

Equipment:

  • Kitchen aid with icecream attachments (optional)
  • Large bowl
  • Large spatula

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whipping cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Mix ingredients completely in Kitchenaid icecream bowl

Notes:
